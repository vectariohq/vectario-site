
# Vectario — Lean Business Plan

## Overview

Vectario is a senior-led cloud, software, and AI engineering consultancy focused on building reliable, maintainable systems for organizations operating in complex or regulated environments.

We specialize in delivering high-quality solutions quickly, while ensuring clients can confidently own and maintain the finished product if they choose.

## Target Clients

- Early-stage and growth-stage companies
- Teams in healthcare, finance, logistics, and data-heavy domains
- Organizations not ready for full-time engineering hires
- Teams needing short-term senior expertise to unblock delivery

## Services

- Cloud architecture, modernization, and cost optimization (Azure-first)
- Backend and distributed systems engineering
- AI enablement (RAG, internal tools, secure LLM integration)
- Data pipelines, event-driven systems, and observability
- CI/CD, infrastructure as code, and delivery acceleration

## Engagement Models

- Fixed-scope starter engagements
- Hourly or short-term advisory
- Ongoing retainers for maintenance and iteration
- Build-and-handoff or build-and-operate models

## Differentiation

- Senior-only execution
- Emphasis on clean, maintainable code
- Transparent communication and scoped work
- Strong testing and operational discipline

## Go-To-Market

- Direct outreach to newly formed or growing companies
- AI-assisted discovery of new businesses
- Referrals and repeat engagements
- Clear, credible positioning via vectario.com

## Operations

Vectario currently operates as a solo-led consultancy with the ability to expand through trusted partners as demand grows.

## Risks and Mitigation

- Feast/famine consulting cycles → mitigate with retainers
- Scope creep → mitigate with clear statements of work
- Over-customization → favor reusable patterns and tooling
